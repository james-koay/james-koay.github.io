Welcome traveller!

7th September 2022

Deep in the forest you stumble upon a clearing where a timber cottage stands humbly alone.

As you approach you notice the decaying wooden walls blistered with white mould.

Shrubs and weeds overgrown right up to the doorstep.

This is no-doubt, a home long abandoned.

You enter and find an old journal. Some pages torn and tattered.

It seems to be the writings of a researcher whom once lived here.

It describes his findings of a nearby bog and how it strangely calls to him.

As you flip through the pages the writing gets increasingly messy and chaotic.

Scrawled at the end are the words: BEWARE. BEWARE THE BLOG BOG BLORG.
